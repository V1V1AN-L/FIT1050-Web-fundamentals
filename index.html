Our Specials

Happy Hut is the one-stop shop for all your needs. From food order services to All-Filipino groceries! All meals on our Lunch Specials and Party Orders are cooked fresh daily or when ordered, so rest assured your meals are not stale or old when served. With Happy Hut’s wide array of services, it is definitely your home shop away from home.

  • Nilagang Baka / Baboy

    A simple dish that has different ways of preparation. Usually served during rainy and cold weather, Nilagang Baka / Baboy is a beef or pork soup composed of different vegetables such as carrots, potatoes, and cabbage.

  • Binagoongan

    Another Filipino favourite aside from Adobo, binagoongan is pork that is stewed and sautéed in shrimp paste, which is made from small shrimps and salt for added flavour. The dish is then garnished with slices of chili before eating.

  • Daing na Bangus

    Milkfish that is marinated overnight in vinegar, garlic, crushed peppercorn, and salt. Afterward, fish is fried in a pan until crispy and golden brown. Daing na Bangus is a staple on Filipino tables as it is one of the breakfast favourites.

  • Chicken Tinola

    An authentic Filipino dish that has chicken as the main star of the dish. Its soup is composed of onion and ginger and is often placed with green papaya wedges or chayote and chili pepper leaves.
